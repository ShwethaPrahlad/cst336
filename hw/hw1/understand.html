The Understand stage is primarily for research, to develop an understanding about the users, their needs, and to understand their perspectives and problems. The two phases that fall into this bucket are Empathize and Define.


Empathize

This phase comprises research, observing, and understanding the users.


Define

This phase comprises noting putting together the research and observations, and defining the actual problems of the user.
